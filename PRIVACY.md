# SnapMacros Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 17, 2026*

SnapMacros ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

## 1. Meal photos & AI analysis

When you take or pick a meal photo for AI analysis, the image is sent to **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash** via Google's generative language API. Google processes the image to estimate food items and macros, then returns the result. We do **not** retain the image server-side — we receive only the AI's JSON response, then discard it.

Google's processing of images submitted via their API is governed by the [Google APIs Terms of Service](https://developers.google.com/terms) and [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

If you do not provide an API key, or the API is unavailable, SnapMacros falls back to a local heuristic estimate — no image leaves your device in that mode.

Meal photos you log are stored on your device only, inside the app's private sandbox. Delete any meal in the Home tab to remove its photo.

## 2. What we collect

| Data | Where it lives | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Weight, height, age, sex, goal | Local device only | Compute your TDEE and macro targets |
| Meal log & macros | Local device only | Daily totals, trends, streaks |
| Subscription flags | Local device only | Unlock paid features after purchase |

We maintain no user accounts. We cannot see any of the above.

## 3. Advertising identifiers (AdMob)

On the free tier, Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers to serve ads. Opt out via:

- **iOS** — Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.
- **Android** — Settings → Google → Ads.

## 4. In-app purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple or Google. We receive only a transaction identifier and product ID — never your payment information.

## 5. Children

SnapMacros is not directed to children under 13.

## 6. Your rights

- **Delete your data**: uninstall the app. All local data is removed.
- **Delete individual meals**: tap the … menu on any meal tile.
- **Opt out of ads personalization**: OS-level toggles above.
- **Contact us**: nalhamzy@gmail.com

## 7. Changes

We'll update this page when the policy materially changes.
